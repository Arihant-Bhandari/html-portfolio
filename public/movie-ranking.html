The Best Movies According to Arihant

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Matrix

Follows the story of a dystopian world where robots have taken over Earth and infringe on human survival.

A Beautiful Mind

Follows the story of a young man who suffers from schizophrenia and deals with it on his journey to become one of the renowened mathematicians of his time.

Maze Runner

Follows the story of a young man thrown into an unfamiliar environment structured like a maze, wherein he blends into the local group and figures outthe mysteries of his arrival.
